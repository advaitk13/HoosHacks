ABOUT US

We are an organization that strives to provide a platform for Hoos to help other Hoos!

Our Goal: Our goal is to help students find a cheap and easy way to clean up after their fun and to also help students make some quick and easy cash on the side.

How It Works

  1. If you have a mess you want cleaned, submit an order by clicking on "Find a Cleaner" at the top of the site to find someone to help you out soon
  2. If you want to make some money, click on apply to be a cleaner, set your prices and hours of avaliablity, enter your phone number, and wait for a job

Anyone who is a UVA student or faculty member can use our application. Private residences only. We are looking to expand to other locations, reccomend areas by contacting us!
